Vendor Security Review Summary

Page 1

This sample document is fictional and safe for portfolio testing. It describes
how a small operations team reviews a vendor before adopting a document
automation platform.

The review found that the vendor supports role-based access, audit logging,
and encrypted transport with HTTPS. The vendor also publishes a data retention
policy and offers a manual data deletion process on request.

Page 2

The main risks are operational rather than technical. The team should confirm
who can approve vendor access, how long uploaded documents remain available,
and whether support staff can view customer files during troubleshooting.

The recommended next steps are to request a security questionnaire, validate
the deletion workflow, and run a limited pilot with non-confidential documents.
The pilot should avoid regulated, financial, or personally identifiable data.
