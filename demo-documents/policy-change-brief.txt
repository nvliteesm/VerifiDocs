Policy Change Brief

Page 1

This sample document is fictional and safe for portfolio testing. It summarizes
a proposed internal policy change for handling shared project files.

The policy requires teams to label project files as public, internal, or
restricted. Public files may be shared in open team channels. Internal files
may be shared with employees working on the project. Restricted files require
manager approval before sharing.

Page 2

The policy creates three implementation tasks. First, project leads must review
existing folders and assign labels. Second, the IT team must update onboarding
guidance with examples for each label. Third, managers must review restricted
sharing requests within two business days.

The primary risk is inconsistent labeling during the transition period. The
mitigation is a two-week review window, followed by a short audit of recently
shared folders.
